Meet the Artist ^_^

Michelle Lugo

The artist goes by Agent Wolfie but thier given name is Michelle Lugo! They were born and raised in Miami Florida with Dominican parents. They currently are attending thier second year in Cuny Manhattan community college for a degree in Animation. Their interest began when they were young, playing the GameCube and Xbox. They are constantly influenced by early 2000s video games such as Sonic the Hedgehog and Jet Set Radio.

Michelle is self-taught and pursued college for the joy of it. Michelle creates their designs using a program called Nomad Sculpt.The characters frequently dress in baggy, stylish clothes and eat food.They mostly publish their artwork on the Instagram app. They have collaborated with several musicians on album artwork and visual effects! Their color palette primarily consists of green, purple, and pink.
